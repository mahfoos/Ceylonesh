Marble Beach


It is also known as marble bay beach. It is situated in Trincomalee Natural harbour area. Marble beach is overlooked by the diamond hill. This beach is located in a stunning environment with grass lands, resting huts, rocks, resorts and it is a calm beach with crystal clear blue water. Considered as one of the wonderful place to be visited in Sri Lanka which admires the minds of foreign and as well as local tourists.
