Software engineering undergraduate

I am Naduni, and I am 20 years old. I schooled at Panadura Sumangala Balika.
I have played the Development role of Student 1 for this Website

As part of the first task assigned to me I had to prepare the presentation page
with the title and the names of everyone in the group. After 4 seconds,
a second page will be displayed connecting to Task 1 of Student 2.

Task 2 involved using JavaScript to create the functionality to increase / decrease
the font size used on the website. It was also required that basic functionality
should include two buttons - a button to make the font size smaller and a button
to make the font size larger. Advanced functionality will allow for the
font size to increase in steps up and down.

It was also required that the site contain a form to allow people
to buy products related to the topic of our website including:
Personal details;
At least 3 different products to choose from;
A number of items for each product;
An automatically generated total price of the bill;
A JavaScript validation to check that the user has filled
compulsory fields (i.e - personal details, product and number of items).
It was also required that I not use HTML5 validation for this requirement.
Additionally, it was also required that the compulsory fields are filled-in,
when the 'place order' button is clicked, where the user would get a pop-up
window with the summary of the order (Eg:- the text in the popup window
could be something like: "Dear John, you have ordered 2 T-shirts size M at a cost of £20
each and a pair of trainers at a cost of £50. Your total bill is £90.")
